Mental Health Information

Understanding mental health helps us support ourselves and others.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of fear or worry that can affect daily life. It may cause symptoms such as rapid heartbeat, sweating, restlessness and difficulty concentrating. Managing anxiety can include breathing exercises, mindfulness and seeking professional support.

Depression

Depression is a common mental health condition that causes persistent sadness and loss of interest in activities. People experiencing depression may feel tired, hopeless or struggle with motivation. Support from friends, therapy and healthy lifestyle habits can help manage depression.

Self Care Tips

Self care includes activities that support emotional and mental well being. Examples include meditation, exercise, journaling and spending time with loved ones. Taking breaks and maintaining healthy routines can improve overall mental health.
